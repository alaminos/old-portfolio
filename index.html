Hi, I'm Antonio. I come from a background in Foreign Language Education and Customer Service, I decided to change my carrer and I am now working (and studying) as a web developer, thanks to an education programme called "alternance" (equivalent to "apprenticeships" in the UK). That is, I work at a company side by side with experienced staff, and about a week per month I assist classes.

What got you me programming, you ask? Long story short, it was a mix of curiosity, a will to build learning tools, thirst for knowledge and a desire for new challenges.

Qu'est-ce qui m'a améné vers la programmation, tu demandes?
